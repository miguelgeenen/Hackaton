About Us

UNDER THE SEA is a meeting point for people who care about the ocean and its unique ecosystem. People who want to participate in the desperately needed cleanup from plastic and debris. People who enjoy the sea and want others to enjoy it as well - for generations to come. UNDER THE SEA is a platform to find water activities with an impact - clean up while you are enjoying the ocean and share it with your community!
