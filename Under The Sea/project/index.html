Our Project Priorities :

1. Address the massive threats facing our oceans; overfishing, coastal pollution, habitat destruction, water temperature warming and acidification.

2. Fulfil the needs of our global community, both fins and fins off.

3. Support the implementation of the United Nations Sustainable Development Goals and the 2030 Agenda for Sustainable Development.

4. To create more global awareness about these massively important topics.

5. To create a global ocean community that can tackle our current issues across the; North Pacific, Indian Ocean, North Atlantic, South Pacific, South Atlantic and the Mediterranean Sea.
